# Isaac D'Césares

**Researcher · Educator · Builder**
Where cognition meets code.

---

I research, build, and teach at the frontier between technology and human learning. My work lives at the membrane where those two worlds translate each other — emerging technologies become meaningful tools, and educational practice informs how we design intelligent systems.

Currently: Educational Technology Coordinator at [Sesc DN](https://www.sesc.com.br), leading national-scale digital transformation across Brazil. Master's researcher at [PPGI/UFRJ](https://ppgi.ufrj.br), studying multi-agent AI systems applied to education.

Based in Rio de Janeiro.

---

### What I'm working on

**Research** — Multi-agent systems, explainable AI, and adaptive learning frameworks. How autonomous agents can collaborate to solve complex educational problems — not as a thought experiment, but as systems that run.

**Building** — AI platforms, learning management integrations, data pipelines for educational assessment. The kind of infrastructure that institutions actually adopt, not prototypes that live in repos.

**Teaching** — Computational thinking for educators, creative computing networks, AI literacy programs. Translating technical complexity into something practitioners can use without losing what makes it meaningful.
